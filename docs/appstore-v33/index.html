A vault for your money — designed like one.

01 · HOME
Every day, one number.
Your daily allowance, flip-digit clear. Spent / saved / sealed at a glance. The status dial tells you if you're on track before the math does.
02 · HISTORY
A bound ledger of your spending.
Cream paper, ink-on-page. Every transaction, every no-spend day, sealed with a wax stamp. Search and filter without leaving the page.
03 · THE VAULT
Patterns only you can see.
100% on-device AI finds your streaks, highlights anomalies, suggests moves. Your spending stays on your iPhone — always.
04 · MILESTONES
Every habit, a medallion.
Bronze, silver, gold — earned through consistency, not streaks-for-streaks-sake. Each one a quiet confirmation you're building something.
05 · MOVE MONEY
Reallocate in seconds.
Pick the from. Pick the to. Tap the amount. Done. Envelope-style budgeting without the binder.
06 · LOG EXPENSE
Seven taps to tracked.
Flip-digit amount. Instant category. Suggested notes. No bank aggregator, no "connecting to your accounts" spinner. Just money in, money out.
07 · MONTHLY WRAPPED
Your month, as a story.
Five slides, shareable to Stories. What you saved, where it went, what it says about you. Spotify-style, built for finance.
08 · PERSONALIZE
Ten accents. Zero premium gates.
Pick a color that's yours. Applied to every button, chart, and highlight. Free for everyone — color shouldn't cost extra.
09 · SETTINGS
Locked, synced, yours.
Biometric lock. Optional iCloud sync. Export when you want. Delete everything when you need. Your data, your rules — no servers involved.

All screenshots captured on iPhone 17 Pro running iOS 26.2. BudgetVault is a privacy-first personal budgeting app — no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no bank aggregators. "Data Not Collected" on the App Store.
